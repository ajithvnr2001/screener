# 📈 Stock Screener — 
## 🔍 Screener Query

```
Is not SME AND
Market Capitalization > 500 AND
Return over 1month > 0 AND
Return over 1week > 0 AND
Current price > DMA 50 AND
Current price > DMA 200 AND
DMA 50 > DMA 200 AND
RSI > 50 AND
RSI < 70 AND
MACD > MACD Signal AND
MACD > 0 AND
Volume > Volume 1month average AND
Current price > High price * 0.85 AND
Profit after tax > 0 AND
Return on capital employed > 15 AND
Return on equity > 12 AND
Debt to equity < 1 AND
Piotroski score > 5 AND
Sales growth 3Years > 8 AND
Profit growth 3Years > 5 AND
Cash from operations last year > 0
```

## 📅 When to Search

### ✅ Best Times to Run the Screener

| When | Why |
|------|-----|
| **Monday morning** (9:00–9:15 AM) | Weekend gives clean data, no intraday noise. Plan the week's trades |
| **1st trading day of the month** | Monthly return resets, fresh momentum signals appear |
| **After a 2-3 day pullback that bounces** | Stocks that survive a dip and still pass all 21 filters = strongest picks |

### ❌ Do NOT Run

| When | Why |
|------|-----|
| During market hours | Data keeps changing, too much noise |
| Budget day / RBI policy day | Extreme volatility gives false signals |
| NIFTY down 3%+ in a day | Panic selling distorts everything |

## 🟢 When to Buy

### Step 1 → Run screener, get the list

### Step 2 → For each stock, verify before buying

- [ ] Open chart on **TradingView / Chartink**
- [ ] Confirm price is **above DMA 50 & DMA 200** visually
- [ ] Confirm **MACD histogram is green** and growing
- [ ] No major news event coming (earnings, AGM, etc.)
- [ ] **NIFTY/SENSEX** is not in free-fall

### Step 3 → Entry Timing

| Entry Type | When to Enter |
|------------|---------------|
| 🎯 **Pullback Buy** (safest) | Price dips 1-2% intraday but stays **above DMA 50**. RSI around **50-55**. This is lowest risk |
| 🎯 **Breakout Buy** | Stock closes at new **weekly high** with **strong volume**. Confirms strength |
| 🎯 **Ideal Entry** | Price is **near DMA 50** (acting as support) + RSI 50-55 zone |

### ⚠️ Do NOT Buy

| Situation | Reason |
|-----------|--------|
| Stock already up **5%+** today | You're chasing — bad entry price |
| RSI is **above 70** | Overbought — wait for a dip |
| Volume is **very low** | No conviction behind the move |
| **All at once** | Split into **2-3 buys** over 3-5 days |

## 🔴 When to Sell

### 🛑 Stop-Loss (Non-Negotiable)

| Rule | Action |
|------|--------|
| Price **closes below DMA 50** | **EXIT** — short-term trend broken. No hoping, no waiting |
| Down **8-10%** from buy price | **EXIT immediately** — capital protection first. Set as **GTT order** on day of purchase |

### 📈 Profit Targets

| Target | Action |
|--------|--------|
| Stock up **15-20%** | Sell **30-40%** of position. Move stop-loss to buy price (now risk-free) |
| Stock up **20%+** | Use **trailing stop** — trail 8-10% below highest price reached. Let winners run |

### 🔴 Technical Exit Signals

| Signal | Action |
|--------|--------|
| RSI goes **above 80** | **Book profits** — overbought |
| MACD crosses **below Signal** | **EXIT** — bullish momentum has died |
| DMA 50 crosses **below DMA 200** | **EXIT everything** — Death Cross |
| Price closes **below DMA 200** | **Full exit** — long-term trend broken |
| Volume spike + **big red candle** | **Exit** — distribution (smart money leaving) |

### 🔄 Re-Entry Rule

> If you sold a stock and it appears in the screener again next week:
> 1. Wait **2-3 days** to confirm the signal is real
> 2. Only re-enter if it passes **all 21 filters** again
> 3. Buy at a **pullback near DMA 50**, not at the top
